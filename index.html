Projects

Below are the projects that I am currently working on.

BCA Chat

This is a website in development that allows the students of BCA to chat/ connect with each other. It also allows BCA students to meet other students with similar interests and to ultimately make new friends.

RaiseMyGPA

This is a website in development that allows BCA students to see what grades they need in each class in order to raise their GPA to a particular amount.

InspireMe

This website is a social media site where people can post projects that they're working on in order to inspire other people with their ideas. They can even be inspired themselves!

Charitable

This is a social media site meant for charities and good causes. People can follow other charities and make donations to them.

Paper Organizer App

This is an app in development that allows people to take a picture of important papers/ documents and organize them and say when they are due/ when they have to be completed by.
